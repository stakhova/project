Our Story

Aman dgtl was created in 2016 by bringing together two in-house departments at Aman group.
The User Experience department, established in 1990, and the Development department, established in 1998; together they provide support throughout the full product cycle, from consulting to execution. And product life cycle. Today, Aman dgtl, a leader in its field, works with local and international companies and organizations in the areas of banking, retail, telecom, insurance, travel, complex systems and more.
